Salmon Avocado Roll

Ingredients:

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori
  • Fresh salmon
  • Avocado
  • Rice vinegar


Salmon Avocado Rolls

Steps:

  1. Prepare rice and season it with rice vinegar.
  2. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo mat.
  3. Spread an even layer of rice on the nori.
  4. Add salmon slices and avocado in the center.
  5. Use the bamboo mat to tightly roll everything together.
  6. Cut the roll into bite-sized pieces with knife.
